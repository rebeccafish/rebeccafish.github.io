Financial services mobile app

These wireframes were made for a major financial services company in NYC that specializes in online stock brokerage. The company was looking to completely overhaul its mobile app.

The new app needed to display the user's accounts and portfolio. It also needed to provide a number of research tools, and allow the user to buy and sell stock.

The final version of the product has recently been released into the iTunes App Store.

Parametric search

This parametric search was designed for a website that provides a vast database of resources for business owners.

The goal was to simplify their filtering system without compromising its power.
