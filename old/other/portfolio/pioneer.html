The Pioneer

Visit Site

2010

Role: UI/UX • Visual Design • Front-end & Back-end Development

Tech Specs:HTML, CSS, LESS, JQuery • Wordpress, PHP, MySQL database

Clean and versatile

The Pioneer, the weekly student newspaper of Whitman College, needed a fresh online home for its news stories, columns, photographs, and illustrations.

I designed a clean, colorful layout with a grid structure reminiscent of the multi-column layouts of traditional newspapers. The Pioneer’s beautiful artwork was a focal point of the site.

Award-winning result

The result was a finalist for the "Online Pacemaker" award, the highest honor a university newspaper website can win from the Associated Collegiate Press (ACP). The site has also won 1st place in the "Best in Show" (2012, 2011) convention competition each year since its redesign.

ACP is the United States' oldest and most prestigious university newspaper organization.

Flexible and easy to update

The site was designed to be flexible and easily extendable. Student newspaper sites have complete staff turnover every four years, so their websites must be able to survive frequent directional shifts.

Powered by Wordpress

Wordpress provides an easy browser-based interface for Pioneer staff to add/edit articles and article metadata.

By taking advantage of the template-based nature of Wordpress, I made sure it was possible for editors to add additional standalone pages or even entirely new sections without HTML.
