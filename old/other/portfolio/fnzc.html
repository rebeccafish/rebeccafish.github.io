Lighter & brighter

First NZ Capital (see also OneView) needed a new look for its online presence.

The business thought that that the site's previous iteration felt too somber. We wanted the new design to feel fresh and modern, but also appropriate for a finance house of its stature.

Bold slideshow

First NZ Capital is based exclusively out of New Zealand, and its intimacy with the local financial market is one of its competitive advantages. To emphasize the firm's Kiwi history, we built the home page's focal point around the beauty of the NZ countryside.

Thinking ahead

The website was designed on a flexible grid, and suits a wide variety of screens. A future iteration will bring full responsiveness to the site.

It's mobile friendly, and the front-page slideshow responds to swipe gestures.

The homepage and navigation

The website has three types of content: static pages about the firm, a few news articles, and the walled "My FNZC" section that was not part of the redesign.

We used the homepage to promote fresh content (mostly news) and popular static info pages, as measured by Google Analytics.

We maintained the old internal page structure. Navigation follows a consistent pattern: 1st tier horizontally across the top of the site, 2nd tier vertically down the left, and third tier breadcrumbs horizontally across the top of the content page area.
